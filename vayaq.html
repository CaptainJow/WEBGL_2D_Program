WEBGL UYGULAMASI

this program allows you to control the object YA using your Keyboard!!!
You can Turn the object to the RIGHT using the "D" key , and You can turn it to the LEFT using your "A" key.
You can also change the COLORS using the "R" Key .
And for Fun You can click on the PARTY!!! button and to stop the animation you can re-click the button !!
